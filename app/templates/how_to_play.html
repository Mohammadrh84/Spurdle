game guide

How to Play

Test how well you really know your favourite artists' music. Try guessing to see how long it takes you to get it right.

step 1

Select Your Artists

Select between 1 to 10 artists. Each round, you will be given a random song from one of your selected artists to guess.

Only songs where your selected artist is the credited creator are included, however, songs where they are a feature on another artists' songs are not included.

You can change your artists at any time via the Artist Select option in the navigation bar, or after each round from the results pop-up.

⚠ heads up

If you select niche/small artists, the played songs may be inaccurate. Artists sharing a name with others may also have each others songs played.
To experience the game as intended try selecting artists that are more established.

step 2

Guessing

As you type a song name, an autofill dropdown will appear to help you just in case. You may occasionally see the same song name appear twice, however, any matching name will be treated as correct.

Guesses are case-insensitive ie., "GOD'S PLAN", "god's plan", and "gOd'S PlAn" are all treated as the same answer.

Misspelt guesses are counted as incorrect. You have a maximum of 15 guesses per round.

important

Scores

If you wish to give up, there is a "Give up?" button at the bottom right of the game screen, underneath the Reveal Hint button.

can't think of it?

Once a guess is made or a hint is used the game is added to your account as played. If you don't finish the round, it will still be counted. This prevents users from closing the game to avoid harming their game statistics when they don't know a song.

scoring

Points

You start each round with 100 points. Every guess and hint will reduce your score. The fewer you use, the higher your score!

Each guess

-3 points

Hint 1 - Release date

-5 points

Hint 2 - Album cover

-10 points

Letter reveal

-15 points

As you play and make guesses, the board updates showing which letters are in the song name and which are not.

A Not used
B In song
C Not in song

Hint 4 - Album name

-10 points

Hint 5 - song snippet

-15 points

A random 2-second snippet of the song plays to help you identify it.

⚠ Artists with limited songs

If an artist has fewer than 10 songs as the main artist (ie, not a feature), you can still play the game as usual but your statistics won't be affected.
This prevents users from exploiting smaller artists to gain easy points.

Good luck and enjoy!
